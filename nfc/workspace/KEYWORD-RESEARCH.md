## SERP landscape (Florida, loc 21142)

### "dumpster repair" / "roll off container repair"
Only small specialty shops & welders — **no national authority to outrank. Attainable now.**
1. containerservicesunlimited.com — "Dumpster Repair & Refurbishing"
2. dumpsterrepairshop.com — "Waste Container Repairs, Dumpster Repair Services"
3. americanshieldcrs.com — "Mobile Container Repair — We Come To You" (mobile angle ranks)
4. ajscontainerrepair.com · con-fab.com · ssmcsteel.com — small fab/repair shops
(plus facebook/mapquest filler)

### "used dumpsters for sale" — N/A but informative
Marketplace-locked: facebook, equipmenttrader, ebay, machinio, ironplanet, rbauction. A small site
cannot beat these organically — this intent is won on **GBP/Maps**, not blue links. (Moot here; no sales.)

### "garbage truck repair" / "roll off truck repair"
Mix of diesel/commercial truck shops (truckrepairorlando, dieselrepairorlandofl, rolloff.com Tampa) and —
tellingly — **containerservicesunlimited.com ranks here too.** Same shop ranking for BOTH containers and
trucks = exactly NFCTR's model. Validates a combined containers-and-trucks positioning. Low comp, winnable.

### "trash compactor repair" — confirmed trap
SERP is AHS, YouTube, iFixit, KitchenAid, PartSelect — **home kitchen appliance DIY.** Do not target the
head term. Target **"commercial compactor repair"** only (different, high-$ intent).
