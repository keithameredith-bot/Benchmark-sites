Fleet Account Questions, Answered

Straight answers — the same ones we'd give you at the counter.

How do I set up a fleet account?

Call us at (352) 421-5650 or stop by the shop and ask about a fleet account. Tell us what you run — how many vehicles, what kind, how hard they work — and we'll set up scheduling and billing that fits your operation.

How many vehicles do I need to qualify?

There's no magic number — if your business depends on vehicles, it's worth a conversation. From a couple of work trucks to a few dozen vans, we'll tailor it to your operation.

What services do fleet accounts cover?

Everything we do: tires, oil changes, brakes, batteries, A/C, tune ups, diagnostics, and general repair — for trucks, vans, and cars, foreign and domestic.

How does billing work?

One account for the business with consolidated billing and a clean service history per vehicle. Talk to us when you set up the account and we'll match it to how your bookkeeping works.

Can you keep our maintenance on a schedule?

Yes — that's the whole point. We track what each vehicle needs and when, schedule around your work week, and flag what's coming due so breakdowns don't set the schedule for you.
