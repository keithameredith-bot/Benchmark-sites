What We Check

Common Culprits Behind a Rough-Running Engine

Ignition & Spark

Worn spark plugs, a faulty ignition coil or distributor, blown fuses, or shorts in the wiring harness.

Fuel & Air Flow

Clogged fuel injectors, a clogged air filter, or an old fuel filter starving your engine of what it needs.

Fluids & Sensors

Low coolant, brake, or transmission fluid; loose belts; a weak battery; or malfunctioning sensors throwing codes.
