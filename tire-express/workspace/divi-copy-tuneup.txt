Think your car or truck is running a bit rough? Has your check engine light come on? A tune up could do the trick to get you running right. The timing could be off, the spark plugs could be bad, your coil could be old. There's lot of reasons your motor might not be running like it used to. Consistent oil changes and service keeps your motor running well but sometimes things go wrong and that's where a tune up comes in.
What All is Involved With a Tune Up?
Our expert mechanics will run a thorough diagnostic on your vehicle. Once we diagnose the issue we will go over your options to remedy the issue. Here's a list of common issues that could be keeping your engine from running as smoothly as it should. For a more inclusive guide on car maintenance check out this consumer report.

Clogged fuel injectors
Clogged air filter
Worn spark plugs
Old fuel filter
Damaged or faulty ignition coil or distributor
Loose belts
Faulty battery
Blown fuses
Shorts in your wiring harness
Malfunctioning sensors
Low fluids, (coolant, brake or transmission fluid)

How Quick Can I Get a Tune Up in Ocala?
It depends on two things. How quick it takes to diagnose the problem, and how quick it takes to fix. Oftentimes its something very simple that can make your motor run rough. A sensor, a filter, a loose wire. These are quick fixes! Call or come in for a quick diagnosis!

Why has my check engine light come on? It could be a number of reasons, we can hook the computer up to your vehicle to determine the reason.

How long does a tune up take? Depending on the level of tune up and subsequent service, it can take anywhere from 2 to 4 hours.
