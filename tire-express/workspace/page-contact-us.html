Before You Call — Quick Answers

The logistics questions we get most.

Do I need an appointment?

No — walk-ins are welcome. An appointment is recommended so we can get you in quicker and give you a solid time estimate.

Can I get a quote over the phone?

For tires and parts, yes — call with your tire size or part and we'll quote you on the spot. Troubleshooting a problem needs a diagnosis first so the quote is honest.

Can I leave my vehicle overnight?

Absolutely — the lot has 24/7 surveillance, so your vehicle is safe with us overnight. Drop the keys and we'll call you when it's ready.

My car won't make it to the shop — now what?

We're partnered with local towing companies. Call us and we'll point you in the right direction and be ready when it arrives.
