Smart, Not Cheap

A good used tire isn't a gamble — if it's been inspected properly. Every used tire we sell gets checked for tread depth, sidewall condition, age, and past repairs before it ever touches your wheel. If we wouldn't run it on our own truck, we don't sell it.

When Used Tires Make Sense

One tire ruined by road debris and the other three still have plenty of life? Matching a single used tire beats buying a full set. Budget's tight this month? A set of inspected used tires gets you rolling safe without the new-tire price. Selling the car soon? Don't put new rubber on a vehicle that's leaving the driveway. And when new tires are the right call, we'll tell you that too — we sell those as well, so we have no reason to push you either way.

What We Check Before We Sell

Tread Depth

Measured, not eyeballed — so you know how much life is actually left in the tire before you buy it.

Sidewall & Damage

Cracking, bubbles, plugs in the wrong place, or hidden damage — any of those and the tire doesn't make the rack.

Age & Repairs

Rubber ages even with good tread — we check the date code and any past repair work before a tire gets our OK.

Used Tire Questions, Answered

Straight answers — the same ones we'd give you at the counter.

Are used tires safe?

An inspected used tire is. The danger with used tires is buying blind — unknown age, hidden damage, bad repairs. Every used tire we sell is checked for tread depth, sidewall condition, date code, and past repairs first. If it doesn't pass, we don't sell it.

How much do used tires cost?

It depends on the size and how much tread is left, but you'll typically pay a fraction of the new-tire price. Call with your size and we'll quote you out the door — mounted and balanced included.

Do you have my size in stock?

Stock changes daily, so call ahead with your tire size (it's printed on the sidewall — like 225/65R17). If we don't have it today, we may have it this week.

Can I buy just one used tire?

Absolutely — that's one of the best uses for them. If one tire is ruined and the other three have life left, we'll match the size and get you back on the road without buying a set.

Should I buy used or new?

Honest answer: it depends on your situation, and we sell both — so we have no reason to push you either way. Keeping the car for years? New usually wins long-term. Need a match, a stopgap, or a budget-friendly set? Used makes real sense.

Do you offer a discount for veterans or first responders?

Yes. Veterans, active military, and first responders get 20% off every service — just let us know when you check in. Thank you for your service.
