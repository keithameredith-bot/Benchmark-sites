New Tire Questions, Answered

Straight answers — the same ones we'd give you at the counter.

How much do new tires cost in Ocala?

It depends on your vehicle and the tire, but we carry options for every budget — from affordable sets that get you safely back on the road to premium Michelins built to last. Because we shop multiple suppliers instead of being locked into one chain's pricing, we can usually beat the national stores. Call us with your vehicle and tire size at (352) 421-5650 for an honest, no-obligation price.

Can you mount oversized tires on my lifted truck?

Yes — lifted trucks and oversized, all-terrain, and mud-terrain tires are something we handle regularly. We'll mount and balance larger-diameter and wide fitments properly so your truck still rides smooth on the highway, and we'll give you honest input on what works for your build before you buy.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Both work. Walk-ins are always welcome, and most tire installs are done the same day. If you'd rather lock in a time, give us a call and we'll set it up.

How long does it take to install new tires?

For most vehicles, a full set is mounted and balanced in about 30 minutes — many customers are in and out without leaving their car. Larger or specialty fitments can take a little longer.

What tire brands do you carry?

We carry Michelin, Goodyear, and Toyo, plus quality mid-tier brands like Continental, Cooper, and Falken and budget-friendly options. If you need a specific tire we don't stock, we'll source it — or buy your tires online, ship them to us, and we'll install them.

Where are you located?

We're at 1708 E Silver Springs Blvd in Ocala, FL 34470 — centrally located on the east side, easy to reach from Silver Springs and Belleview. Open Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
