The Science, Simply

What Refrigerant Does & Why It Runs Low

It Absorbs Heat

Refrigerant absorbs the heat from the air inside your cabin — your A/C system transfers that heat out using it. Less refrigerant, less cooling.

Small Leaks Add Up

Over time, tiny leaks in the system let refrigerant escape — and your A/C gets gradually less effective without you noticing.

Recharge Usually Fixes It

Most of the time a recharge does the trick. If the cold only lasts a short while, that points to a bigger leak — and we can find and fix it.

Not Just a Top-Off
