Shop & Scheduling

Getting in, getting quoted, getting back on the road.

Do I have to make an appointment?

No, but it's definitely recommended — it gets you quicker service and a better expectation of how long your repair may take. Walk-ins are always welcome.

Can I just get a quote?

Sure thing — give us a call and we can quote tires or parts on the spot. If you're troubleshooting a problem, we'd need to diagnose the vehicle first so the quote is honest.

How long will the repairs take?

That's entirely up to the level of the job — but our estimates are usually pretty accurate. We have a comfortable lobby, or you can leave your vehicle with us.

Can I leave my vehicle overnight?

Absolutely — our shop has 24/7 surveillance, so your vehicle is safe staying with us overnight.

Do you provide towing?

We're partnered with local towing companies to get your vehicle to us — call and we'll point you in the right direction.

Do you have financing options?

For certain items like tires and wheels, we have Affirm available — get what you need today and split it into payments.

Parts, Tires & Repairs

The hands-on questions.

Will you install auto parts that I buy myself?

That's not a problem. Whether it's tires, wheels, or another part, we charge you just for the install time.

Do you repair tires?

Yes — and properly. We do inside patch-plug repairs, not just a quick plug to get you by, and we'll tell you straight when a tire can't safely be saved.

Why does my car smell musty when I use the A/C?

It's possible you need a new cabin filter! It's often overlooked — your vehicle's air conditioning system has a filter just for your cabin air. If the air's also not blowing cold, see our A/C repair & recharge page.

How do I know the repairs will be done right?

Our mechanics are certified experts and we make sure the job is done correct. If you experience any issues, we're always just a phone call away.

How big of a tire can you handle?

We balance up to 38 inches — and we'll mount and install bigger. Lifted trucks and off-road setups are kind of our thing.

Do you work on fleets?

We love serving fleets — one local shop, one account, less downtime. Give us a call to see how we can keep your fleet serviced and running well.
