Common Questions, Answered

Straight answers — the same ones we'd give you at the counter. Don't see yours? Call us.

Do I have to make an appointment?

No — walk-ins are welcome. An appointment is recommended though, so we can get you in quicker and give you a better expectation of how long the work will take.

Will you install tires or parts I bought myself?

Not a problem. Tires, wheels, or another part — bring them in and we'll charge you just for the install time. Buying tires online? You can even have them delivered straight to the shop.

Do you have financing options?

Yes — for tires and wheels we offer Affirm financing, so you can get safe rubber today and split the cost into payments.

Do you provide towing?

We're partnered with local towing companies to get your vehicle to us — call and we'll point you in the right direction.

Can I leave my vehicle overnight?

Absolutely. Our shop has 24/7 surveillance, so your vehicle is safe staying with us overnight.

Can I just get a quote?

Sure thing — call and we can quote tires or parts on the spot. If you're troubleshooting a problem, we'd need to diagnose the vehicle first to quote it honestly.

How do I know the repairs will be done right?

Our mechanics are certified experts and we make sure the job is done correct. If you ever experience an issue afterward, we're a phone call away.

How long will repairs take?

Depends entirely on the job — but our estimates are usually pretty accurate. Wait in our comfortable lobby or leave the vehicle with us.
