The Internet Sells Tires. We Install Them Right.

Online tire prices can be hard to beat — but a box of tires in your garage doesn't get you anywhere. We're the local shop that finishes the job: professional mounting, balancing, TPMS service, and disposal of the old set. Buy wherever the deal is. Install where the equipment is.

How It Works

1. Buy Your Tires

TireRack, SimpleTire, Amazon, a buddy's garage sale — wherever. Bring them in, or call us about shipping them straight to the shop.

2. Book a Time

Call ahead and we'll have a bay ready. Most installs are done while you wait in our comfortable waiting area.

3. Drive Out Done

Mounted, balanced, TPMS checked, torqued to spec, old tires recycled. The deal you found online, finished properly.

Worth Knowing Before You Buy Online

Double-check the size on your sidewall before you order (like 225/65R17 — we'll happily confirm it if you call). Make sure the seller's road-hazard terms make sense, since online warranties usually mean shipping a tire back. And if you're between two choices, call us first — we sell tires too, and sometimes our out-the-door price beats the online deal once shipping and install are counted. Either way, you'll get a straight answer.

What "Installed Right" Actually Means

Mounting a tire isn't the hard part — doing it without scratching your wheels, getting the balance perfect, and handling the TPMS sensors correctly is. That's the difference between a shop install and a "guy with a machine" install.

Tire Installation Questions, Answered

Straight answers — the same ones we'd give you at the counter.

Will you install tires I bought online?

Yes — gladly, and with zero attitude about it. Bring them in (or ask about shipping them straight to the shop), book a time, and we'll mount, balance, and install them properly.

How much does tire installation cost?

It depends on the tire size and whether TPMS service is needed — call with your size and we'll quote you out the door. No surprises when you pick up the keys.

How long does an install take?

Most sets are done while you wait. Call ahead and we'll have a bay ready for you.

Do you handle TPMS sensors?

Yes — we check and reset your tire pressure monitoring sensors as part of the install, so you don't drive off with a warning light glowing.

Can you install big off-road tires?

Balanced up to 38 inches, mounted bigger — lifted trucks and off-road setups are kind of our thing. Done properly, which matters double at that tire price.

Do you offer a discount for veterans or first responders?

Yes. Veterans, active military, and first responders get 20% off every service, including installs — just let us know when you check in. Thank you for your service.
