# FGS — Photo Shot List for the Client

> THE ONE RULE: **shoot everything HORIZONTAL (landscape), wide-angle, phone sideways.**
> Every photo we have is vertical — vertical photos cannot fill the wide banner slots on the
> site without ugly crops. 0.5x/wide lens on a modern iPhone is perfect. Midday overcast or
> morning light beats harsh noon sun.

## Priority 1 — fills empty/duplicated slots today
1. **GPR cart on a slab or driveway** (only service page with zero photos) — operator pushing it, wide.
2. **Truck-mounted drill rig, mast UP, on site** — the signature geotech shot. Several angles, wide.
   (We have video of it but it's too low-res for stills.)
3. **Lab, wide room shot** — benches, load frame, ovens, someone working. The current lab photo is a
   vertical close-up doing double duty on two pages.
4. **Headshots: Ryan + Dave** — outdoors on a job site in FGS shirts beats studio. Chest-up, landscape OK.

## Priority 2 — upgrades existing banner crops

9. Open sinkhole or excavation WITH a person and equipment in frame for scale, shot wide from the side
   (our current ones are straight-down verticals).

## Priority 3 — content/marketing
