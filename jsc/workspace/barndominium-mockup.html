What Are The First Steps to Building a Barndominium?

The first step to building your barndominium is finding a floorplan. Folks often spend countless hours scrolling through the internet for the floor plan that strikes them just right! We encourage this, and yes we can build anything that you find! No job is too big or small for us.

Want a customized floorplan? This is possible as well! You can draw out what you are looking for and we will have one of our architects bring your ideas to life. Or just tell us what you're wanting and we can work one up from scratch! Find some plans online that are close but not quite what you want? Adjustments are possible as well.

Remember our slogan at JSC is "You dream it. We build it."

What's Next After Plans?

You've got your plans, but now you need to think about materials! Your materials list can swing the cost of your barndominium quite a bit. The price of windows, doors, garage doors, countertops, flooring. All of these items and more need to be figured out for a solid quote. It's also not just materials. It's choices like do I want an outdoor kitchen? A hot tub? Landscaping, sprinkler systems, driveways, we do it all at JSC!

If you're not using financing it's easy for us as a builder to work with you throughout the build. We can establish up front what items you want to shop for and what items you would like us to order! If you are using financing we really need to nail down all of your materials options and appliances so you have the total complete cost of the build ready for your lender. Lenders typically don't allow for you to come out of pocket for items during construction.

Let's Break Ground on Your Barndominium!

Breaking ground is a great feeling! Before we can finalize the quote for your barndominium we need to know where you're building. We are based out of central Florida but we can build throughout the state, so that's not the problem. Every building site is different and requires a different level of site prep. We want to make sure that your barndominium is elevated properly. We need to check soil conditions, and there may be some clearing to do. Proper site prep is crucial for building a barndominium. This is one of the things that makes us the best barndominium builders in Florida.

A barndominium can have a large footprint, combining both your home and barn space for some builds. This requires a perfectly prepared pad for construction. It ensures the stability of the site, and the soundness of your structure.

Do you have a lot you own already? Or do you need to buy land? Are you planning on financing the build? If so purchasing your land at the commencement of construction is something you may want to consider. Land loans require heavy down payments compared to construction loans which are possible with as little as 5% down. We can guide you through this process with one of our preferred lenders.

Constructing Your Barndominium From The Ground Up

This is where you're going to see the JSC crew shine. We show up and get the job done. Your barndo may be metal framed or use poles but we start there along with the foundation. Once it's cured your trusses go in. We quickly aim to dry your structure in with a roof. Then we install your siding, metal or otherwise. Then we move to windows, exterior doors, and insulation.

With your barndominium dried in and protected from the elements we can then begin interior construction. We start to frame out your rooms, using lasers and other state of the art construction advancements we make sure every wall, line, and trim is precise and straight.

Electrical, plumbing, air conditioning all get installed in coordination. JSC prides itself on superior construction management. This ensures our builds are completed in a timely manner.

Drywall and or wood siding gets installed. A lot of barndominium owners are looking for a rustic look. Stressed metal interior siding, stone, and aged wood are common additions!

Final touches include your flooring, kitchen, bathrooms, lighting, appliances, and any outdoor items you've chosen as well.
